🥞 Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour

  • 1 cup milk

Steps:

  1. Mix everything
  2. Pour into a pan
  3. Cook until golden

🍫 Chocolate Mug Cake

A super quick and delicious dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps:

  1. Mix everything in a mug
  2. Microwave for 1 minute
  3. Let it cool

  1. Blend everything
  2. Serve cold
